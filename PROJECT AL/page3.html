Who played the iconic roles of the movie?

The actors and actresses of "A Star Is Born"

Janet Gaynor as Esther Blodgett / Vicki Lester

Janet Gaynor portrayed the character Esther Blodgett, who later becomes Vicki Lester. Gaynor was a prominent actress of the era, known for her expressive acting style and versatility. Her performance in "A Star Is Born" was widely praised for its depth and emotional resonance, capturing the struggles and triumphs of an aspiring actress in Hollywood.
